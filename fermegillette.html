Research

La Ferme Gillette is a dairy farm located in Eastern Ontario. They are a family owned company. They have 400 cows which are well known for their high gentics. They were also the home of the world record cow; Smurf. Which produced more than 57000 gallons of milk in 15 years.

Their perception by others is friendly, caring, family oriented, hardworking and well treated animals. They are looking for a logo that is welcoming and professional.

Sketches

Solution

When gathering research, the company was looking for a professional logal that would show the friendliness they have to offer. Keeping in mind that they are a dairy farm was very important. With this information, when thinking of dairy, cows and milk are the first things that came to mind. Many sketches were created using cows until the appropriate one was formed.
