Research

Many people drive cars but know nothing about them. Having an app that simplies car maintenance would help them a lot. No one enjoys worry about their car and wasting time at the shop.

Pocket Mechanic is an app for any who owns a vehicle. It is a maintenance tracking app to help users save money and stay safe. The app offers 24 hour access to the levels and important warning lights associated to the vehicle. Pocket Mechanic eliminates unneeded trips to the mechanic. It also allows you to track your loved ones and make sure they have a working vehicle.

Solution

The main goal was to create a simple app that is easy to navigate and understand for types of drivers. The main features are to track fluid levels and be alert is there are any issues. The main page offered cards with easily identifable icons with a bar indicating remaining fluid. The cards could then be pressed for more information. When there is an important issue with your vehicle, an alert would be sent. The user could choose to ask for more help or dismiss the alert.
