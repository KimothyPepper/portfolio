--- layout: default --- Skate Spot

Research

There are many people who love to skate, they could be hockey players, figure skaters, familys and friends.

Having an app to help them locate various indoor or outdoor rinks would simplify their activities. The app would offer then information about each rink such as hours and how busy they are. The included features are a map, list of rinks and list of favourites.
